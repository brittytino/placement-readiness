## Prompts Used During Development

1. Explain how K-Means Clustering works from scratch in Python.

2. Implement Euclidean distance calculations without using Scikit-learn.

3. Design a K-Means algorithm that performs centroid initialization, cluster assignment, and centroid updates iteratively.

4. Analyze the time complexity and space complexity of K-Means Clustering.

5. Generate a visualization of clustered two-dimensional data points using Matplotlib.

6. Document the architectural design and Big-O trade-offs of the implementation.
